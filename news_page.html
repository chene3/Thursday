Important Notice: E3 Road Construction Underway in Thursday

1999.02.21

Attention all Thursday residents and visitors: Construction on E3 Road will begin next week, and is expected to continue for the next several months. The road will be undergoing extensive renovations and upgrades, including repairs to the pavement and sidewalks, as well as the installation of new streetlights and traffic signals.

During the construction period, E3 Road will be partially closed to traffic, with only one lane open in each direction. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause, and we ask that all drivers exercise caution and patience when navigating the construction zone.

Pedestrians and cyclists are advised to avoid the area if possible, and to take alternate routes when necessary. Signage will be posted throughout the area to direct traffic and provide information on detours.

We appreciate your patience and cooperation during this important construction project, which is designed to improve the safety and functionality of YISHEN Road for all residents and visitors to Thursday. We will continue to provide updates on the progress of the construction and any changes to the traffic flow as they become available.

Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to the completion of this important project in the coming months.
