Church

The ancient church in the town is a historical landmark with architectural traces from the last century. It offers worship services every weekend, welcoming visitors to participate with a pious heart and witness the beauty of the historical building.

Fountain

The tourism center provides visitors with information about town tours, transportation, and lost and found services, among other assistance.

Alistair Thursday

Over a thousand years ago, the town of Thursday was founded by a powerful wizard named Alistair Thursday. To commemorate his legacy, a magnificent sculpture of him has been erected, which stands as a testament to the town's rich history and magical heritage.

Tourism center

The tourism center is responsible for providing information and guidance for visitors to the town. They offer suggestions for things to do and see in the area.

Mysterious Lake

The mysterious lake is said to attract many wolf packs on the nights of the full moon, and some believe that strange and mystical events occur around the lake.

School of Thursday

Founded by Alistair Thursday, the School of Thursday is a wizarding school with a history spanning several centuries. The school has produced some of the most powerful wizards in history, and while the mystical powers have gradually weakened and become lost over time, the School of Thursday still holds records of those ancient powers. The school offers visitors an opportunity to experience a magical flight in the sky, like a wizard.

Crystal Cave

Legend has it that there is a mysterious crystal on Mount Crystal that contains powerful magic. Every year, hundreds of explorers come to the mountain in search of the crystal, but no one has found it yet.

Shadowpaw

There is a mysterious magical cat that lives in the wilderness forest, and no one has ever seen its true form. However, whenever someone in the town encounters the cat, good things seem to happen.

Transportation Information

Public Transportation

  • Bus: The city has an extensive bus network, with routes covering all major areas. You can find route maps and schedules at the bus station or online.
  • Subway: The subway system is fast and convenient, with trains running from early morning until late at night. Fares are based on distance traveled.

Car

  • Car Rental: There are several car rental companies in the city. You can rent a car with a valid driver's license and credit card.
  • Parking: There are paid parking spots throughout the city. Be sure to check the parking regulations in the area you're visiting.

Bicycle

  • Bike Sharing: The city offers a bike-sharing program with stations located throughout the city. You can rent a bike with a credit card or through the program's mobile app.
  • Private Bike: If you have your own bike, you can use bike lanes on the road or ride on the sidewalk outside of busy areas.

Pedestrian

  • Bike Sharing: The city offers a bike-sharing program with stations located throughout the city. You can rent a bike with a credit card or through the program's mobile app.
  • Private Bike: If you have your own bike, you can use bike lanes on the road or ride on the sidewalk outside of busy areas.
